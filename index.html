Crystal Collector Game

Instructions

• You will be given a random number at the start of a game
• There are four crystals below, by clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score
• You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random
• The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it
• Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change
